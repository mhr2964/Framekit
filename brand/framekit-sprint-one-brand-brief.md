# Framekit Sprint-One Brand Brief

This document is the authoritative sprint-one brand brief for Framekit. It consolidates the finalized homepage copy and the compact microcopy packet for room creation and review.

---

## Homepage copy

### Hero

**Feedback, without the workflow.**

Share a room, add your work, and collect focused comments in context — without turning review into a bloated process.

**Primary CTA:** Start a room  
**Secondary CTA:** See review flow

---

### Value prop section 1

#### Keep feedback on the work
Framekit puts comments where they belong, so teams can respond to what’s actually on screen instead of chasing context across threads, docs, and calls.

---

### Value prop section 2

#### Lighter setup, faster review
Create a room in moments, invite people in, and start gathering feedback right away. Less admin, less ceremony, more momentum.

---

### Value prop section 3

#### Clear for reviewers, calm for teams
Reviewers can jump in without friction. Teams get a clean stream of actionable feedback instead of noise, overlap, and status clutter.

---

### Closing CTA

#### Review should move work forward
Framekit gives you a simpler way to share work, collect feedback, and keep things moving.

**Primary CTA:** Start a room  
**Secondary CTA:** Create your first review

---
