## 1) Messaging hierarchy

### Recommended primary headline
**Create, review, and share work without the usual review workflow drag.**

### Recommended primary subhead
Framekit gives small teams and freelancers a faster async path from first frame to focused feedback to share-ready handoff — with less ceremony, clearer context, and less manual coordination.

### Recommended primary angle
**Async collaboration that moves quickly without getting messy.**

This best combines the approved speed-to-output language from the waitlist package with the sprint-one brand wedge against bloated review workflows. It makes the create → review → share sequence legible while keeping the promise grounded in clarity and handoff simplicity.

### Value props
1. **Create without setup drag**  
   Start a review room quickly, add the work, and move straight into feedback without heavy process.

2. **Review in context**  
   Keep comments on the work so feedback stays clear, actionable, and easy to follow asynchronously.

3. **Share with less handoff friction**  
   Move from in-progress work to a review-ready link that others can open and respond to without extra coordination.

4. **Keep momentum between passes**  
   Framekit helps teams move from rough starting points to a clearer next version faster.

5. **Replace workflow sprawl with one guided flow**  
   Instead of stitching review across threads, docs, and calls, Framekit keeps the path simple: create, review, share.

### Backup angle variant A
**Feedback, without the workflow drag.**  
Create a room, collect focused comments in context, and hand work off cleanly.

### Backup angle variant B
**From first frame to share-ready review, faster.**  
Framekit helps teams create, review, and share work through a lighter async flow.

### Guardrails
- Preserve approved themes: guided flow, speed to output, clarity/consistency, less manual friction
- Use the anti-incumbent wedge explicitly: no bloated workflow, less ceremony, less admin
- Avoid unapproved claims: AI specifics, integrations, quantified performance, enterprise/admin features
- Mark any UI-specific or proof-dependent statement as **Provisional**
