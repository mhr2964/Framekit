# Framekit Launch Brief

## Product truth for v1
Framekit helps creators run fast async creative review with dedicated review rooms, simple invites, and comments in one place.

## Primary audience
Creators and small creative teams who are tired of chasing feedback across DMs, docs, and live calls.

## Core message
Create a review room, invite collaborators, and collect comments without the usual review chaos.

## Primary value props
- Faster feedback loops
- Less scattered review work
- Simple collaborator invites
- Clear comments tied to the review room

## Primary CTA
Join the waitlist

## Approved language
Use:
- async creative review
- review rooms
- invite collaborators
- comments and feedback in one place
- faster feedback
- less back-and-forth

## Forbidden language
Do not say or imply:
- public share links are live
- open/public review pages
- anonymous external review
- anyone-with-the-link access
- share-by-link as a current feature

## Roadmap note
If needed, mention share-link workflows only as a future v1.1 roadmap item, clearly separated from the main value prop and CTA.
